Classical Dances

Indian Heritage

  • Bharatanatyam (Tamil Nadu) Graceful, powerful movements; strong footwork and expressive facial gestures. Often tells stories of Hindu gods.
  • Kathak (North India) Known for fast spins, rhythmic footwork, and storytelling. Influenced by Mughal culture.
  • Kathakali (Kerala) Dramatic dance with elaborate makeup and costumes. Uses bold facial expressions and eye movements.
  • Odissi (Odisha) Soft, flowing movements inspired by temple sculptures.
  • Kuchipudi (Andhra Pradesh) Combines dance and drama, usually performed as storytelling.
